PHYSICS FORMULAS IMPLEMENTED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. KEPLER'S LAWS (All 3)
   • Law of Ellipses: r = a(1-e²)/(1 + e·cos(ν))
   • Law of Equal Areas: Same area swept in equal time
   • Harmonic Law: T² ∝ a³
   
2. ORBITAL MECHANICS
   • Orbital velocity: v = √(GM/r)
   • Escape velocity: v_e = √(2GM/r)
   • Orbital period: T = 2π√(a³/GM)
   • Mean anomaly: M = 2π(t-t₀)/T
   
3. GRAVITATIONAL PHYSICS
   • Newton's law: F = G·m₁·m₂/r²
   • Gravitational constant: G = 6.67430 × 10⁻¹¹ m³/(kg·s²)
   
4. ORBITAL ELEMENTS (6 Parameters)
   • Semi-major axis (a)
   • Eccentricity (e)
   • Inclination (i)
   • Longitude of ascending node (Ω)
   • Argument of perihelion (ω)
   • Mean anomaly (M)
   
5. EQUATION SOLVERS
   • Kepler's equation: M = E - e·sin(E) [Newton-Raphson]
   • True anomaly: tan(ν/2) = √((1+e)/(1-e)) × tan(E/2)
   
6. 3D TRANSFORMATIONS
   • Rotation matrices for orbital elements
   • 3D inertial frame positioning
   • Position vector calculations
   
7. ADVANCED CALCULATIONS
   • Tidal force: ΔF = 2·G·M·R/r³
   • Hill sphere: d = a·(m/M)^(1/3)
   • Roche limit: d = 2.456·R·(M₁/M₂)^(1/3)
   • Orbital resonance: T₁/T₂ = p/q
